ABOUT PAGMT
The Palo Alto Girls Math Tournament (PAGMT)
is a math competition for middle school girls.
Hosted by a group of Paly students, we hope
to inspire prospective mathematicians with
a challenging yet enjoyable competition.
Check out Contest Page for more information.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
WHERE WILL PAGMT BE HELD?
PAGMT will be held in-person at Palo Alto High School's MAC building on May 9th, 2025. The contest will take place between 5:00 PM and 8:30 PM.
WHO CAN ATTEND?
PAGMT is free to attend and is open to all middle school girls, not just limited to PAUSD Students. Highly motivated elementary students should also come.
WHAT IS THE CONTEST FORMAT?
The contest will be a 60 minute long individual exam. Problems will be free response and will cover algebra, geometry, combinatorics, probability, and number theory.
WHAT SHOULD I BRING?
Contestants may use writing utensils and erasers. Notably, rulers, compasses, protractors, calculators, or any other computation device is not allowed. Blank paper will be provided during the competition.
